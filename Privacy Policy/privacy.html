We collect various types of information from users to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you register, apply for a job, or create a profile, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, address, resume, and employment history.

The information we collect is used for:

We only share your information with third parties in limited circumstances, such as:

  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law, or in response to a legal request, to protect our rights, users, and safety.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account or personal information, please contact us. Note that some information may be retained as required by law.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in those emails.
